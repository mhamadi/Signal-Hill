Journey Through the Signal Hill Historic Site

Signal Hill, St. John's, NL

Signal Hill is one of the most breathtaking attractions located in St. John's, Newfoundland. Signal Hill recalls the town's historic past as well as offering coastal hikes and colourful performances against sweeping views overlooking the Atlantic.

Take a Hike

There are several hiking trails at Signal Hill National Historic Site, all of which boast panoramic views and captivating scenery.

Amazing sites nearby

There are numerous beautiful sites close to Signal Hill that you can't miss to visit. One of them is the easternmost point of North America. Here are the top three sites:

  • Cape Spear Lighthouse National Historic Site
  • Castle Hill National Historic Site
  • Hawthorne Cottage National Historic Site

Pack Accordingly

One of most important things when it comes to traveling through the great outdoors is packing accordingly. Here are a few tips:

  1. Bring layers of clothing
  2. Pack sunscreen
  3. Carry extra water just in case
  4. Pack light
